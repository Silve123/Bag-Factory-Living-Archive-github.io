Diana Hyslop

Diana Hyslop is based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Working largely with oil, acrylic and digital mediums, Diana Hyslop’s work draws on the human experience, traversing our emotions and spirits, our vulnerability and our defenses. Within many of her works she portrays an individual who has adopted a totem, which represents the uncontrolled instincts and ideas that effect our perceptions.
