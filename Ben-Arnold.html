Ben Arnold

Ben Arnold is a South African Postwar & Contemporary artist who was born in 1942. Ben Arnold’s work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 91 USD to 200 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2016 the record price for this artist at auction is 200 USD for Seated Figure, sold at Russell Kaplan Auctioneers in 2023.
